## Mission

Obtain **full legal authority for Faresay to operate in every US state (50 + DC)** — i.e. the
company is properly formed/qualified, the clinical-services structure is lawful in each state,
data handling is compliant, and the 15% platform-fee model is structured so it does not breach
fee-splitting / corporate-practice rules anywhere Faresay operates.

## Objectives (in priority order)

1. **Retain US healthcare-regulatory counsel** who can validate the operating structure
   (CPOM / fee-splitting / MSO–PC) across multiple states. *This is objective #1 — it gates
   everything below.*
2. **Decide the entity & clinical structure** with counsel (US parent, MSO, friendly PC where
   required) and form the necessary entities.
3. **Establish compliance foundations:** HIPAA posture + BAAs, state health-privacy laws,
   provider-licensing verification process, telehealth modality compliance.
4. **Qualify to do business in each state:** foreign qualification / certificate of authority +
   registered agent in all 50 states + DC; any state-specific telehealth/out-of-state
   registrations.
5. **Stand up ongoing compliance:** annual reports, license monitoring, renewals, insurance
   (professional liability, tech/cyber E&O).

## Authority granted to the agent

- **May:** research; identify and shortlist attorneys/vendors/registered-agent services;
  draft all correspondence; prepare ready-to-send drafts; request quotes (without committing);
  organise trackers and logs; propose meetings.
- **Must get Joel's per-item approval before:** sending any message; submitting any form or
  filing; spending any money; agreeing any term, fee, scope, deadline, or meeting; signing or
  accepting anything.
- **Must never:** commit Faresay to an obligation, share confidential/legal-sensitive material
  externally, or make a legal determination on its own.

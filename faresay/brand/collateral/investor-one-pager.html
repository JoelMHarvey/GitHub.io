Investor brief · confidential

Fair, private practice software for UK therapists.

B2B SaaS the therapist subscribes to — not a marketplace. Free to start, monetised by a small card commission and paid tiers, UK-first and privacy-by-design.

Problem

UK therapists are stuck choosing between clunky generalist software, expensive marketplaces that take a cut and own the client, and a pile of spreadsheets. None are calm, fair, and built for them.

Solution

One simple tool — clients, scheduling, secure video, payments, reminders, compliance — that the therapist owns. They keep the client relationship and the record; we’re the processor, never the middleman.

Model

Subscription (Starter free · £39 · £79) + a small card commission (2.5 / 1 / 0%). Recurring + usage, no marketplace risk.

Market

Tens of thousands of UK private-practice therapists (BACP/UKCP/NCPS), growing demand for accessible mental-health care.

Moats

UK-specific compliance + PMI invoicing, fairness/ownership trust, opinionated simplicity, switching costs as the practice runs on it.

Status

Faresay Ltd incorporated (England & Wales, 17302034). Product live and UK-launching: onboarding, verification, payments (Stripe Connect), secure video, reminders, compliance + outcome tooling shipped. Bootstrapped; recruiting founding therapists. Legal/DP pack drafted; ICO registration applied.

Why now

Cost-of-living + NHS waitlists push more care to affordable private practice; therapists want fair, modern, UK-compliant tools.
